The North East Feast

Ae the world we live in today becomes more and more complex, we tend to forget the things that we dont do on a repetative basis. One thing we have all come accustomed to is going to the super market and getting our food pre-packaged and ready to eat.


We believe that these simple skills are dying out and need to be practiced more and more by everybody. We welcome you to join us as we go over multiple different concepts that will feed your family for genorations to come.

What are we going to include:

  • Hunting and processing of wild game.
  • Fishing both fresh water and saltwater.
  • Forraging the wilderness for edible plants and mushrooms.
  • Small scale backyard agriculte agriculture.
  • Cultivating crops in small and large scale.
